Mobile Lift Containment System

Product Overview

As the saying goes, "Necessity is the mother of invention." The concept for the mobile lift containment system originated from a significant project known as "Project Mustang," the largest manufacturing plant of its kind globally, near Denver International Airport. Thousands of metal structures were exposed to the elements, causing primer deterioration. Our system prevents flaking and potential contamination of food and beverage production lines.

Problem

Market Gap

Before this invention, significant man hours were spent covering and tarping equipment below spray areas.

Customers
